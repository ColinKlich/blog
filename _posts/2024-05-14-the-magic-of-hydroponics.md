## What exactly is hydroponics?
Hydroponics is a soil-less method of agriculture or gardening that relies on water and nutrient solutions to nourish plants. It originates from the Greek words for water and work, indicating its reliance on water-based cultivation.
Scientists have determined that [just 16 nutrients](https://university.upstartfarmers.com/blog/overview-hydroponic-nutrient-management) are required for plant growth. So, by circulating water containing these essential nutrients and growth hormones around plant roots, hydroponics recreates the natural environment of plants, achieving efficient and accelerated growth.

## Benefits of Hydroponics:
**Accelerated Growth:** Experience the thrill of faster plant growth with hydroponics. By providing direct access to nutrients without the plant having to search through soil, growth rates can be 2-3 times faster than traditional gardening methods.
**Water Efficiency:** Hydroponic systems use up to 90% less water than traditional farming, as water is recirculated in a closed loop system.
**Space Savings:** Stackable growing systems mean you can grow more in less space — perfect for urban environments or small homes.
**No Pests or Diseases:** Without soil, you eliminate many common soil-borne pests and diseases, reducing the need for pesticides.
**Year-Round Growing:** Control your environment and grow crops year-round, regardless of outdoor weather conditions.
